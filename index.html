About Me

I am recent graduate of UCSD with BS in computer Science. I enjoy building web application and projects that is relevant to my educational background and knowledge set. I would like to expose myself with challenging task that will help me become a better software engineer.
Teambased software development is the most exciting and intellectually engaging activity I have participated in. As part of my Advanced Software Engineering class, I was assigned to work with a group of 60 students on a web based checkin application that involved utilizing software engineering principles. As member of the Engineering subteam, my primary duty was to implement the dashboard that performed simple CRUD operation to keep track of the appointments made by patients. I used Express JS, a Javascript framework for web apps to communicate between a client and the server. I also have worked on the web based banking application using Mean Stack. I wrote a features like signup page, transfer money from one account to another account and closing account.
I have been very active in my pursuit of becoming a software engineer. I’m part of Tau Beta Pi (TBP), an engineering honors society, where I was able to enhance my leadership skills, improve my service learning skills and gain fresh ideas from engineering students of all disciplines. My journey has just started and there is so much left to learn. By pursuing this job, I hope to use cutting edge technologies, understand how to approach complex problems, and work along with experience engineers.
